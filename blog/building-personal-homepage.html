Building a Personal Homepage

Published: September 14, 2025

Set up this homepage today as a simple hosting platform for things I'm building. The goal is straightforward: when people stumble into my work, they can find what they're looking for and engage appropriately.

Purpose

This site is primarily to host my personal projects, collections, and thoughts. It's a digital workspace where I can quickly upload and organize:

  • Memes and visual content (organized topically)
  • Reference materials and links
  • Personal photos and art
  • Short thoughts and aphorisms
  • Drafts and work-in-progress items

Design Choices

Used a lab-themed design inspired by my pdoom1 game project. Dark color scheme with lab-style panels and buttons. Static HTML/CSS/JS for simplicity and speed - no complex frameworks needed.

Structure

Built with a simple multi-page structure. Each section gets its own page with consistent navigation. Assets organized in topical folders for easy local management.
